Our Mission

Helping Climate Change: Our company focuses on bringing anyone, especially low-income families, energy through cheap solar panels. We want to make this form of energy more popular than the toxic and harmful fossil fuels we use every single day to help save our environment, and make it accessible and less confusing for anyone of any background.

What We Do

Our company, Helios, is a solar panel company dedicated to serving low- to middle-income families by providing sustainable and affordable energy. Through our website, interested homeowners can view the eligibility requirements they must meet and submit a form to apply.
